About festival

The 10th annual Boston Film Festival is organised by the company Watch it outside and will be held in Boston Public Garden this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight. The aim of the Festival is to raise awarenessand promote international cinema in all its forms as art, entertainment and as an industry, in a spirit of freedom and dialogue.
